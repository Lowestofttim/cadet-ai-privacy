Cadet AI — Privacy Policy

Study aid for UK Army Cadet Force (ACF) & Combined Cadet Force (CCF) cadets aged 13+

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Cadet AI (“the app”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is a study aid for UK Army Cadet Force (ACF) and Combined Cadet Force (CCF) cadets. We take the privacy of young people seriously and have written this policy to be clear and honest about what we collect and why. This app is not an official MOD, ACF, CCF or Cadet Forces product.

Operator / data controller: Cadet AI  ·  Privacy contact: support@cadetai.co.uk

1. Who this app is for (aged 13 and over)

Cadet AI is for cadets aged 13 and over. You must be at least 13 to create an account: when you sign up we ask your age, and if you are under 13 we do not create an account for you — we ask you to have a parent or guardian help instead. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13; if we learn we have, we will delete it.

Parents and guardians can contact us at the address above at any time to see, correct, or delete their cadet’s data.

2. What we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work:

  • Account details — your email address (via Google sign-in or email/password sign-up). We never see or store your Google password.
  • Profile you create — display name, cadet organisation (ACF/CCF), section (e.g. Army/RAF), rank, star level, and the unit or school you select.
  • Age check — a record that you confirmed you are old enough to use the app (we store a yes/no “age verified” flag, not your date of birth).
  • Your progress — quiz results, scores, XP, badges, awards, streaks, and rank.
  • Messages to TANGO — the questions you type to the in-app AI assistant and its replies, so the feature can answer you and keep your chat history.
  • App settings — your preferences (theme, reminders, leaderboard opt-in, etc.).

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising. The app has no advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics trackers, and we do not sell your data.

3. Microphone

If you use TANGO’s optional voice/dictation feature, the app uses your device microphone to turn your speech into text. Depending on your device, this speech-to-text may be handled by your device’s operating system or its built-in (for example, Google) speech service rather than by us. You are asked for microphone permission first, and you can decline or revoke it; the rest of the app still works.

4. How we use your data

  • To create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • To save and show your training progress, badges, awards, and rank.
  • To answer your questions in the TANGO assistant.
  • To show leaderboards only if you choose to appear on them (see §6).
  • To send you the optional daily streak reminder if you turn it on.

Legal basis (UK GDPR): we rely on our legitimate interests in providing the study service you have chosen to use, weighed against your interests as a young person (Article 6(1)(f)); and on your consent for optional features such as leaderboards and reminders (Article 6(1)(a)), which you can withdraw at any time in Settings. In the UK, 13 is the minimum age at which a young person can agree to use an online service like this without a parent.

5. Who we share it with (our processors)

We use a small number of trusted service providers to run the app. They process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy terms:

  • Supabase — secure hosting, database, and authentication for your account, progress, and TANGO chat history.
  • Google — only if you choose “Continue with Google”, to sign you in. Google tells us your email address and basic profile; we do not post anything to your Google account.
  • OpenAI — the messages you send to TANGO are sent to OpenAI’s API to generate a reply (and are also saved as your chat history in our database). Messages sent through the OpenAI API are not used to train OpenAI’s models. Please do not type sensitive personal information into TANGO.

We do not share your personal data with anyone else, and we never sell it.

International transfers. Some of these providers process data outside the UK (for example, OpenAI is based in the United States). Where your data leaves the UK, we rely on the UK International Data Transfer Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (or an equivalent safeguard, or transfer to a country the UK recognises as providing adequate protection) so that your information keeps an equivalent level of protection.

6. Leaderboards

Leaderboards are off by default. If you turn on “Appear on leaderboards” in Settings, your display name, unit/school, and XP can be shown to other cadets in national and unit rankings. You can turn this off at any time, and we will stop including you.

7. How long we keep it

We keep your account and progress data while your account exists. Your TANGO conversation history is kept in your account until you delete it or delete your account. If you ask us to delete your account, we delete your personal data (we may keep minimal records where the law requires).

8. Your rights & deleting your data

Under UK GDPR you can ask us to: access a copy of your data; correct it; delete your account and all associated data; restrict or object to how we use it; or receive it in a portable format. To make any of these requests — including deletion — email the privacy contact above; we will respond within one month. A parent or guardian can make a request on a cadet’s behalf. You can also complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at https://ico.org.uk.

9. Security

Your data is sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored by our hosting provider (Supabase), which encrypts data at rest, with access controls limiting who can see it. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information and will act promptly if we become aware of a personal-data breach.

10. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the “Last updated” date above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app.

11. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns: support@cadetai.co.uk.
